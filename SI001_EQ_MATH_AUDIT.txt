SI001 EQ MATH AUDIT

Reference: Robert Bristow-Johnson Audio EQ Cookbook
Filter Type: Peaking EQ
A = 10^(gainDb/40)
omega0 = 2*pi*f0/Fs
alpha = sin(omega0)/(2*Q)

Coefficients (Direct Form I/II):
b0 = 1 + alpha*A
b1 = -2*cos(omega0)
b2 = 1 - alpha*A
a0 = 1 + alpha/A
a1 = -2*cos(omega0)
a2 = 1 - alpha/A

Normalization: All b and a coefficients are divided by a0.
Magnitude Evaluation: Evaluated via H(z) transfer function on unit circle z=exp(jw).
Cascade Reasoning: dB magnitudes are added (Equivalent to multiplying complex transfer functions).
